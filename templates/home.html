How It Works

  1. Employees submit their preferred working days and any time off
  2. Managers define team collaboration requirements
  3. Administrators set organizational constraints (office capacity, minimum in-person days)
  4. The solver optimizes schedules balancing all constraints and preferences
  5. Everyone sees their personalized schedule and understands how their preferences were accommodated
